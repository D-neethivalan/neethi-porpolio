Case study

Solar installation campaign — self-initiated project

I researched real objections solar buyers have, then built a full Meta Ads campaign around them — from customer pain points to five finished ad creatives.

Ad copy framework used

Hook

"Planning to install solar but not sure what happens in the rainy season?" — opens with the objection buyers were already thinking.

Lead

Reframes rainy season as only 3–4 months a year, then anchors the value: bill drops from ₹10,000 to ₹2,000, plus up to ₹1,00,000 in government subsidy, handled end-to-end.

Body

Builds trust with 10+ years of experience and in-house manufacturing — removing the middleman objection.

CTA

Form-fill offer: 10% off plus a free solar product for the first 10 customers, to create urgency without discounting the core price.
